> The world of news is noisy, because they have to hype it. They try to get you to pay attention to something that’s not actually important. They create a false sense of urgency, social status, fear, shock, or any tricks possible to manipulate your psychological triggers, and ultimately help them profit.
- linked page How to Live, linked page Derek Sivers

And I know what you are going to think about it, because I am thinking in the same way. But quite often, it is at most half of the story, while the other half left out by the media could be like
![](../../public/assets/why-i-don-t-read-news/02-d784b8997abc03e0-group_17.png)

And it is part of human nature to [Jump into conclusions](./jump-into-conclusions.md), so the news will always win.
