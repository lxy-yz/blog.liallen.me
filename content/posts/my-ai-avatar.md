We’re living in a crazy time that everyone is an AI artist thanks to amazing AI art generators like DALL-E,  Midjourney and etc.

Today, I get caught by an interesting article that shows how to use Midjourney to create Pixar-style AI avatars, as a life-long fan of Pixar fascinated by its [movies](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_Pixar_films) and peculiar [innovative culture](https://www.fastcompany.com/3027135/inside-the-pixar-braintrust).

The good news is that everyone can make their own version of it within 30 minutes. And the generated result looks more than just appealing to me. It’s abstract like an art but tangible enough so that you can tell who’s masked behind the hilarious cartoon face.

Below is the instruction plus a history of my attempts.
1. Choose the image and make it publicly accessible
2. Write the [prompt](https://docs.midjourney.com/docs/parameter-list)
3. Repeat 2 to tune the prompt until you’re happy with the result
